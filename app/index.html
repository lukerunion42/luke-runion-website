Luke Runion

professional bio

The first program I built was an itemized invoice application.

Ever since, I’ve been working to break into the developing world.

Here’s how I got started…

I was born in Nashville, TN. Nashville has been my home for 22 years now. This city is a lot like the development world in that it is rapidly growing faster and faster. After high school, I decided to stay in Nashville to attend college and study Exercise Science at Lipscomb University.

The summer before going into my Junior year of college I had an app idea. I decided to pick up an extra course, “Intro to Computer Programming”, to see what it would take to develop the app. While taking this course I fell in love with programming. It was like solving puzzles all day. I knew while developing code I was also developing a passion for this field and it was a field I wanted to pursue. Instead of changing majors and spending an extra 2 years in school, I decided to switch to an online university where I could graduate with a programming degree. I enjoyed the online university platform and the discipline made me learn how to be self-taught in this field. I graduated from AIU in September of 2018 with a BSIT in Software Development.

Early in my college years, I worked part-time while attending college at a Nashville IT company and then later, I worked full time in the evenings as a server at The Standard at the Smith House, a top rated Nashville restaurant. The service industry has taught me so many great lessons: patience, being respectful of others, prioritizing my time, problem solving, interviewing skills, how to sell, understanding how hard work pays off, thinking on my toes and speaking with confidence in front of complete strangers. I consider these all invaluable life skills.

Graduation has led me to job searching and building this website and portfolio. I’m available for interviews or to answer questions in person, through phone or email, and I can be reached through the Get in Touch button!

Our Features

We’ll Watch the Weather

Download our app and we’ll send you a notice if it’s about to rain in the next 20 minutes around your current location. A good rain can be refreshing, but sometimes you just want to stay dry.

Global Guides

We’ve scoured the entire planet for the best retreats and beautiful vistas. If there’s a corner of the world you want to escape to we know the most scenic and inspiring locations.

Wi-Fi Waypoints

We only send you on trips to places we can personally vouch for as being amazing. Which means we’ve mapped out where local wi-fi spots are and marked them in our app’s map view.

Survival Kit

Everytime you book an escape with us we send you a survival kit with the finest materials. The kit will allow you to setup a tent, start a fire, scratch your own back and lower your taxes.
